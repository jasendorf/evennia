# DorfinMUD — Awtown World Document
*The Land of Dorfin | Hub Town: Awtown*

---

## Overview

Awtown is the starting hub of DorfinMUD, a classic heroic fantasy town with occasional whimsy. It is the seat of the three Founders of Dorfin and the launching point for all adventurers. The town is self-contained, fully explorable, and packed with services, trainers, merchants, and characters.

---

## The Three Founders

| NPC | Real Name | Office | Role | Buff |
|-----|-----------|--------|------|------|
| Jorvyn Malgrave | JM | Malgrave's Parlour | Town Coordinator | **Malgrave's Rally** — boosts Persuasion, Leadership, party morale |
| Marro Hammerfall | MH | Hammerfall's Workshop | Master of Works | **Hammerfall's Blessing** — boosts weapon damage & armor durability |
| Joleth Ondrel | JO | Ondrel's Study | Keeper of Records | **Ondrel's Insight** — boosts XP gain & Lore skill checks |

The three offices sit side-by-side near the Main Entry. Adventurers may visit one, two, or all three before heading out; buffs stack.

## ROADS & PATHWAYS

Roads are named by stretch. When a road changes direction (turns a corner), it becomes a new named road. Intersections are named as Squares or Crossings.

---

### Founder's Walk *(Top E-W Road)*
Runs east-west across the northern interior of Awtown, from The Warden's Gate to The Grand Gate. The most prestigious road in town, flanked by the Founders' offices and key civic buildings.

> *"The cobblestones here are swept clean each morning. You walk where the Founders walk."*

- Each block: **"Along Founder's Walk"** (numbered west-to-east if needed: Founder's Walk I, II, III...)
- Connects: The Warden's Gate → Artificer's Post → Steward's Hall → The Shadow Chamber → [N-S Junction] → Hammerfall's Workshop → Malgrave's Parlour → The Grand Gate

---

### Warden's Way *(Left N-S Road)*
Turns south off Founder's Walk near the west side. Runs north-south through the western interior, connecting the upper civic area to the crafting district.

> *"A quieter road, favored by craftsmen and those who prefer to avoid the bustle of Founder's Walk."*

- Connects: Founder's Walk (north) → Quartermaster's Cache area → The Round Table junction → Craftsman's Road (south)

---

### Templegate Lane *(Interior N-S Road — Central)*
Runs north-south through the center of Awtown, connecting Founder's Walk down past the Temple entrance. Named for the grand temple it serves.

> *"A short but well-worn stretch. Pilgrims, healers, and the merely curious all pass through here."*

- 2 blocks running north-south
- Connects: Founder's Walk (north) → Temple of the Eternal Flame entrance (south)

---

### The Lantern Road *(Interior N-S connecting roads)*
Short north-south road segments that connect Founder's Walk to Market Row through the building interior. Named for the lanterns hung at each intersection.

> *"Small lanterns flicker at every junction. Someone keeps them lit, though no one knows who."*

---

### Market Row *(Middle E-W Road)*
Runs east-west through the center of Awtown, connecting the Temple district to the Vault of Gold and the Outfitter's Rest. The busiest road for commerce.

> *"The smell of fresh bread from the Hearthstone and the clink of gold from the Vault greet you from either direction."*

---

### Craftsman's Road *(Bottom E-W Road)*
Runs east-west across the southern interior, passing the Grand Forge and the Tinker's Den. Dusty, noisy, and smells of metal and sawdust.

> *"The ring of hammers is never far away on Craftsman's Road. Watch your step — there are always crates in the way."*

---

## GATES & ENTRIES

---

### 🚪 The Grand Gate *(Main Entry)*
**Type:** Major gate (orange)
**Connects:** The Eastern Commons ↔ The Herald's Hall / Founder's Walk
**Description:** Awtown's grand eastern entrance. Two iron-banded oak doors stand open during daylight. Guards in polished town livery flank the arch. Carved above the gate: *"Leave lesser than you arrived."*

**NPCs:**
- **Gate Captain Vonn** — gruff, fair, seen it all. Gives new players a brief orientation speech on first entry.
- **Guard Tessa** — younger guard, curious about adventurers, source of local rumors

---

### 🚪 The Warden's Gate *(Side Entry — West)*
**Type:** Side gate (orange)
**Connects:** The Dusty Paddock ↔ Founder's Walk (west end)
**Description:** A smaller but sturdy western gate, primarily used by locals, craftsmen, and those arriving with mounts. Less formal than the Grand Gate.

**NPCs:**
- **Warden Crabb** — old, suspicious, waves through regulars but eyes strangers carefully

---

### 🚪 The South Gate *(Back Entry)*
**Type:** Back gate (orange)
**Connects:** The Garden of Remembrance ↔ Craftsman's Road (south end)
**Description:** A quiet gate at the southern end of Awtown, rarely busy. The ironwork is entwined with carved vines — a tribute to the garden beyond.

**NPCs:**
- **Gate Hand Birch** — young, bored, reads books on duty. Will chat about lore if approached.

## TOWN BUILDINGS & SPACES

---

### 📣 The Herald's Hall *(Receptionist — Welcome Center)*
**Location:** East side, just inside The Grand Gate
**Description:** The first room most adventurers see inside Awtown. High ceilings, bright torchlight, a roaring fireplace. A large Quest Board dominates one wall. Town maps available here.

**NPCs:**
- **Herald Bramwick** — endlessly enthusiastic middle-aged man in official town colors. Greets every new adventurer by name after their first visit. Provides town orientation, hands out starter quests.
- **Scribe Dilly** — Bramwick's assistant. Sells town maps, handles paperwork for new guild registrations.

**Features:**
- Quest Board (interactive — displays available quests)
- Free town map (given to new players on first visit)
- Notice Board (local gossip, rumors, event notices)

---

### 🏛️ The Assembly Hall *(Main Conference Room)*
**Location:** East side, adjacent to Herald's Hall
**Description:** A grand vaulted chamber used for town meetings, public announcements, and formal ceremonies. Rows of benches face a raised speaking dais. Portraits of the Founders hang on the walls.

**NPCs:**
- **Town Crier Aldous** — only present during "town events" (periodic scripted events). Announces news loudly and with great drama.

**Features:**
- Event space — used for special in-game occasions
- Lore plaques on the walls describing Awtown's founding history

---

### 🛍️ The Outfitter's Rest *(Waiting Area — Free Soft Clothing & Simple Items Shop)*
**Location:** Center-east, adjacent to Ondrel's Study
**Description:** A cozy shop with overstuffed chairs by the window. New adventurers can claim a basic starter kit here — simple clothing, a belt pouch, a torch. Nothing fancy, but enough to not arrive naked.

**NPCs:**
- **Shopkeep Marta** — generous, grandmotherly, worries about adventurers going out underprepared. Gives starter gear freely to new players, sells basic goods cheaply to others.

**Features:**
- Free starter kit for new characters (once per character):
  - Simple Tunic, Simple Trousers/Skirt, Belt Pouch, Torch (x3), Waterskin, Hunk of Bread
- Basic item shop (cheap consumables, torches, rope, etc.)

---

### 🏦 The Vault of Gold *(Bank)*
**Location:** Center, adjacent to Market Row
**Description:** A squat stone building with a heavy iron door. Inside, polished wood counters and barred windows give it an air of serious business. The Vault of Gold holds Awtown's currency exchange, storage, and lending services.

**NPCs:**
- **Banker Guildred Copperpot** *(Gnome)* — precise, obsessive about numbers, speaks in decimal. Honest to a fault, absolutely immovable on fees.
- **Vault Guard Holt** — enormous human in chainmail. Doesn't talk much. Very effective.

**Features:**
- Currency storage (player bank account)
- Currency exchange (future: multiple currencies)
- Safe deposit box (item storage)
- Loan system (with interest — future feature)

---

### 🔨 Malgrave's Parlour *(Founder JM — Jorvyn Malgrave)*
**Location:** North side, east cluster
**Description:** A warm, welcoming office that somehow always feels slightly busy. Comfortable chairs face a large desk covered in notes and schedules. A "You've Got This!" pennant hangs slightly crooked above the door.

**NPC: Jorvyn Malgrave**
- Personality: Energetic, personable, knows everyone by name, always has a plan
- Dialogue flavor: "Ah! Perfect timing. I was just thinking about you. Well, someone like you. Are you heading out? Let me give you something to take with you."
- **Buff — Malgrave's Rally:** Temporarily boosts Persuasion, Leadership, and party morale bonuses (1 hour / recharge daily)

---

### ⚙️ Hammerfall's Workshop *(Founder MH — Marro Hammerfall)*
**Location:** North side, east cluster (adjacent to Malgrave's Parlour)
**Description:** Absolute chaos. Every surface is covered in half-built devices, tools, spare parts, and diagrams. Smells of oil and hot metal. The man himself is always elbow-deep in something.

**NPC: Marro Hammerfall**
- Personality: Gruff, warm, says little but means everything he says. Communicates mostly in grunts and gestures while working.
- Dialogue flavor: *[doesn't look up]* "Gear's looking rough. Hold still." *[tightens something on your armor]* "Better. Now go."
- **Buff — Hammerfall's Blessing:** Temporarily boosts weapon damage and armor durability (1 hour / recharge daily)

---

### 📚 Ondrel's Study *(Founder JO — Joleth Ondrel)*
**Location:** Center-east, just off Market Row
**Description:** Floor-to-ceiling shelves of books, maps, scrolls and documents. A meticulous system that only Joleth understands. A single clear desk sits in the center, always with an open book on it.

**NPC: Joleth Ondrel**
- Personality: Quietly brilliant, slightly distracted, warm underneath the absentmindedness. Speaks in complete paragraphs.
- Dialogue flavor: "Oh — yes — I've been reading about the region you're heading to. Fascinating history. The short version? Don't touch the old stones. The long version — I've written it down. It's forty pages. I'll summarize."
- **Buff — Ondrel's Insight:** Temporarily boosts XP gain and Lore skill checks (1 hour / recharge daily)

---

### 🗡️ The Shadow Chamber *(Sensitive Conference Room)*
**Location:** North side, west cluster
**Description:** A plain, unremarkable door that most people walk past. Inside: a round table, six chairs, no windows. Used by the Town Guard leadership and the local Thieves' Guild liaison (not that anyone admits that). Access restricted.

**NPCs:**
- **Spymaster Vex** *(only appears under certain quest conditions)* — lean, watchful, never answers a question directly

**Features:**
- Locked door (Lockpicking skill check, or special key required)
- Source of high-level intrigue/stealth quests

---

### 📋 The Steward's Hall *(Program Manager)*
**Location:** North side, west cluster
**Description:** A tidy administrative office where town logistics are managed. Bulletin boards, ledgers, supply manifests. A place of organized industry.

**NPCs:**
- **Steward Pell** — efficient, no-nonsense woman who manages the town's logistics. Gives supply-run and delivery quests.
- **Clerk Nimble** — young halfling assistant, fast with a quill

**Features:**
- Town delivery quests (fetch/carry type quests for new players)
- Town reputation tracking notice board

---

### 🔧 The Artificer's Post *(Tech Support)*
**Location:** North side, west cluster
**Description:** A bright, cluttered workshop where broken things come to be fixed. Magical items, mundane tools, and odd contraptions in various states of repair line the walls. A friend of Marro's runs this place.

**NPCs:**
- **Tinker Cogsworth** *(Gnome)* — old friend of Marro Hammerfall, talks too fast, incredibly knowledgeable about magical and mechanical items
- **Apprentice Sprocket** — young gnome learning the trade, occasionally breaks things while fixing them

**Features:**
- Item repair service
- Identifies magical items (for a fee)
- Sells tools, components, and odd gadgets

---

### 🙏 The Temple of the Eternal Flame *(Temple) [/4]*
**Location:** Center of Awtown
**Description:** Awtown's grand temple, dedicated to a pantheon of gods favoring light, knowledge, nature, and valor. The four wings each serve a different aspect of the faith.

| Sub-room | Name | Deity/Theme | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|-------------|
| NW | **The Nave** | General worship | High vaulted ceiling, pews, eternal flame at the altar. Main temple room. |
| NE | **The Sanctuary** | Healing & Restoration | Quiet, candlelit. Clerics tend to the wounded here. Healing services available. |
| SW | **The Vestry** | Knowledge & Ritual | Robes, ritual items, prayer texts. Cleric class training available here. |
| SE | **The Bell Tower** | Valor & Protection | Stairs lead upward. Paladin blessings and protective prayers offered on the ground level. A narrow stair continues up to the Bell Tower Upper and, at the very top, the Belfry. |

**NPCs:**
- **High Priest Edwyn Lux** *(The Nave)* — solemn, kind, rarely hurries. Oversees all temple functions. Sells blessings and healing.
- **Sister Sera** *(The Sanctuary)* — enthusiastic young priestess, quick to help, still learning some spells. Provides basic healing.
- **Brother Aldwin** *(The Vestry)* — Cleric trainer. Serious, formal, expects dedication.
- **Paladin-Warden Thane Dusk** *(The Bell Tower)* — battle-scarred retired paladin who now trains others. Gruff but honorable.

**Vertical rooms (up from The Bell Tower):**
- **The Bell Tower Upper** — a landing halfway up the tower. Arrow slits look out over the town. A weapon rack holds consecrated arms. `up` from here leads to the Belfry; `down` returns to The Bell Tower.
- **The Belfry** — the top of the tower. The great bronze bell hangs silent except at dawn and dusk. On a clear day the view rivals The Precipice. No NPC resides here permanently, but Thane Dusk occasionally climbs up to think.

**Features:**
- Healing services (paid)
- Resurrection service (expensive)
- Cleric & Paladin class training
- Blessing scrolls for sale
- Daily "Dawn Prayer" event (small XP/stat bonus for attending)

---

### 🍺 The Hearthstone Inn *(Lunch Room — Tavern) [/4]*
**Location:** Center of Awtown
**Description:** The social heart of Awtown. Warm, loud, perpetually smelling of roasting meat and spilled ale. The four rooms serve different aspects of inn life.

| Sub-room | Name | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| NW | **The Common Room** | Main tavern floor. Crowded benches, a roaring hearth, the bar along one wall. Music most evenings. |
| NE | **The Kitchen** | Run with iron efficiency by Bess. Players with Cooking skill can occasionally use the kitchen. |
| SW | **The Inn Counter** | The lodging desk. A worn counter of dark wood, pigeonholes for keys behind it, a ledger open on top. This is where Bess handles rentals. `rent room` command resolves here. |
| SE | **The Cellar** | Stone stairs lead down to the barrel-lined cellar. Dark, cold, secret passage *may* exist here... |

**NPCs:**
- **Innkeeper Bess Copperladle** *(Common Room / Inn Counter)* — warm, formidable, remembers every face and their preferred drink. The unofficial mayor of the tavern. Handles room rentals at the Inn Counter. Knows more about Awtown's gossip than anyone.
- **Barkeep Finn** *(Common Room)* — quick wit, quick hands, serves drinks without spilling a drop. Source of rumors and bar quests.
- **Lute-player Cobble** *(Common Room, evenings)* — wandering bard, plays nightly, terrible at taking requests gracefully
- **Cook Darra** *(The Kitchen)* — doesn't like visitors in her kitchen. At all.

**Features:**
- Rest/rent room mechanic at The Inn Counter (`rent room` command — restores HP/MP over time)
- Food and drink menu in The Common Room (stat buffs)
- Rumor system (Finn & Bess provide rotating world rumors)
- Occasional Bard events in The Common Room (small XP bonus for attending)
- Hidden cellar — secret passage (for future questline)

### 🔮 The Crystal Repository *(Server Room — Courtyard type)*
**Location:** Center-east, adjacent to The Hearthstone
**Description:** An open stone courtyard enclosed by tall walls. At its center stands a humming crystal formation — an arcane storage device of unknown origin. Scholars come here to read stored knowledge; others find it unsettling.

**NPCs:**
- **Archivist Quellan** — a quiet half-elf who tends the crystals. Speaks to them when he thinks no one is watching. Provides Lore quests and sells Knowledge scrolls.

**Features:**
- Lore lookup (players can query in-world history/bestiary)
- Knowledge scrolls for sale
- Arcane research quests

**NPCs:**

### 📖 The Apprentice Hall *(Interns)*

### 🧙 The Hermit's Hollow *(Wise Hermit)*

### 🗺️ The Cartographer's Den *(Open Courtyard — The Stone Yard)*
**Location:** Central-west courtyard, accessible via orange gate from Warden's Way (WW-3) or east through to The Lantern Road (LR-3).
